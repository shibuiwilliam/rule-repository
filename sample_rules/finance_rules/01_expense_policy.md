# Expense Policy

Company-wide policy governing business expense claims, approvals, and reimbursement.

## General Principles

- Expenses must be necessary, reasonable, and incurred for a legitimate business purpose
- Employees must exercise the same care spending company money as they would their own
- All expenses must be properly documented and approved before reimbursement

## Submission

- Submit expense reports within 30 days of incurring the expense
- Reports older than 60 days require CFO approval
- Reports older than 90 days will not be reimbursed

## Receipts

- Original receipts required for all claims over 1,000 JPY
- Credit card statements alone are not sufficient; itemized receipts are required
- If a receipt is lost, a lost-receipt declaration form must be completed with manager approval
- Electronic receipts (PDF, email confirmation) are accepted

## Approval Matrix

| Amount | Approver |
|--------|----------|
| Under 50,000 JPY | Direct Manager |
| 50,000 - 300,000 JPY | Department Head |
| Over 300,000 JPY | CFO |

## Travel

- Book travel through the approved booking platform when available
- Economy class for domestic flights; business class requires pre-approval for international flights over 6 hours
- Hotel rates must not exceed the per-city cap published by Finance
- Per diem covers meals and incidentals; actual meal receipts are not required when per diem is claimed

## Entertainment

- Record: date, venue, all attendees (names and companies), business purpose
- Per-person limit: 5,000 JPY (tax excluded) for the small-scale exemption
- Entertainment of government officials requires compliance pre-approval
- No entertainment on company card for personal occasions

## Prohibited Expenses

- Personal expenses of any kind
- Alcohol purchases above the entertainment limit
- Gifts exceeding 10,000 JPY per occasion without compliance approval
- First-class air travel without CEO approval
- Spouse/family travel costs
