# 36 Agreement Template (三六協定)

Agreement on Overtime Work and Holiday Work pursuant to Article 36 of the Labor Standards Act.

## Agreement Details

### Article 1 (Parties)
This agreement is entered into between the employer and the labor representative (elected by majority of employees).

### Article 2 (Scope of Overtime)
Overtime work may be required for the following reasons:
- Urgent client deliverables or system incidents
- Seasonal business volume increases
- Month-end and quarter-end closing operations
- Special projects approved by management

### Article 3 (Limits)
- Daily overtime limit: 4 hours
- Monthly overtime limit: 45 hours
- Annual overtime limit: 360 hours

### Article 4 (Special Clause)
In extraordinary circumstances (system outages, regulatory deadlines, M&A transactions), the monthly limit may be extended to 80 hours and the annual limit to 720 hours, subject to:
- Monthly overtime including holiday work must not exceed 100 hours
- Average overtime over any 2-6 month period must not exceed 80 hours
- Special clause may be invoked no more than 6 months per year

### Article 5 (Holiday Work)
Holiday work may be required up to 2 days per month for the reasons stated in Article 2. Holiday work premium is 35% above base wage.

### Article 6 (Health Considerations)
Employees whose monthly overtime exceeds 80 hours must be offered a medical consultation. Managers must monitor and manage overtime hours actively.

### Article 7 (Effective Period)
This agreement is effective for one year from the date of filing and must be renewed annually.
