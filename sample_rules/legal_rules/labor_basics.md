# Labor Standards — Basic Rules

Rules derived from common labor regulations applicable to employee management.
These serve as a starting point for HR/attendance use cases.

## Overtime

Monthly overtime hours MUST NOT exceed 45 hours per employee except under
a valid 36 Agreement (三六協定) on file. Violations expose the organization
to regulatory penalties.

## Rest Periods

Employees working more than 6 hours in a day MUST be granted at least 45
minutes of break time. Employees working more than 8 hours MUST be granted
at least 60 minutes. Break periods must be granted consecutively and during
the middle of the work period.

## Late-Night Work

Employees under 18 years of age MUST NOT be assigned work between 22:00
and 05:00 except in cases explicitly permitted by applicable regulations.

## Annual Leave

Employees who have worked continuously for 6 months and attended at least
80% of scheduled work days MUST be granted a minimum of 10 days of annual
paid leave. Employers MUST NOT deny timely annual leave requests without
compelling operational justification.

## Equal Treatment

Employment conditions MUST NOT discriminate based on nationality, creed,
or social status. Wage differences between employees performing
substantially equal work MUST be justified by objective criteria such as
experience, skill, and responsibility level.
