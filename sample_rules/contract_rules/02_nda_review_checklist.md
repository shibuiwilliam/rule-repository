# NDA Review Checklist

Standard review points for mutual and one-way non-disclosure agreements.

## Mandatory Review Items

### 1. Definition of Confidential Information
- Is it explicitly defined with enumerated categories?
- Are standard exclusions present (public knowledge, independent development, prior possession, third-party receipt)?
- Is it appropriately scoped (not overbroad)?

### 2. Obligations
- Are obligations mutual (for mutual NDAs)?
- Is the standard of care specified (e.g., "same care as own confidential information, no less than reasonable care")?
- Are permitted disclosures defined (employees, advisors, contractors with need-to-know)?

### 3. Duration
- Is there a fixed confidentiality period?
- Is it reasonable for the industry (typically 2-5 years)?
- Does it distinguish between the agreement term and the survival of confidentiality obligations?

### 4. Return/Destruction
- Is there a return-or-destroy clause?
- Does it allow retention of copies required by law or internal compliance?
- Is certification of destruction required?

### 5. Remedies
- Is injunctive relief acknowledged?
- Are attorney's fees addressed?

### 6. Governing Law
- Is it specified?
- Is the jurisdiction reasonable (avoid unfamiliar foreign jurisdictions)?

## Common Issues to Reject

- "Perpetual" confidentiality with no sunset
- One-sided obligations in a "mutual" NDA
- Non-compete or non-solicitation buried in confidentiality clauses
- Assignment clauses allowing transfer to unknown third parties
- Broad residuals clauses that gut the protections
