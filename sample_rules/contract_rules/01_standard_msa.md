# Standard Master Service Agreement (MSA) Guidelines

Review checklist and mandatory provisions for master service agreements.

## Required Clauses

Every MSA must include the following clauses at minimum:

1. **Scope of Services** — Clear description of services, deliverables, and acceptance criteria
2. **Payment Terms** — Fee structure, payment schedule, late-payment interest, currency
3. **Term and Termination** — Initial term, renewal mechanism, termination for convenience (with notice period), termination for cause
4. **Intellectual Property** — Ownership of deliverables, background IP, license grants
5. **Confidentiality** — Mutual obligations, standard exclusions, survival period
6. **Liability** — Limitation of liability cap, exclusion of consequential damages, carve-outs for IP indemnity and confidentiality breach
7. **Indemnification** — IP infringement indemnity, third-party claims
8. **Data Protection** — Personal data handling, DPA requirements, cross-border transfer
9. **Insurance** — Required coverage types and minimums
10. **Governing Law and Dispute Resolution** — Jurisdiction, arbitration vs. litigation

## Approval Authority

| Contract Value | Required Approval |
|---------------|-------------------|
| Under 1M JPY | Department Manager |
| 1M - 10M JPY | Division Head |
| 10M - 50M JPY | Director |
| Over 50M JPY | Board / CEO |

## Red Flags

The following terms must be escalated to legal review:
- Unlimited liability provisions
- Automatic renewal without opt-out notice
- Exclusive jurisdiction in counterparty's home country
- IP assignment clauses covering pre-existing IP
- Indemnification obligations without cap
- Penalty clauses (liquidated damages above 10% of contract value)
