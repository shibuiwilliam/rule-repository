# Code of Conduct

**YourExampleCompany** — IT Service Consultation

**Document ID:** POL-001
**Version:** 2.0
**Effective Date:** 2025-01-01
**Owner:** People & Culture Department
**Approved by:** CEO, Board of Directors
**Review Cycle:** Annual

---

## 1. Purpose

This Code of Conduct establishes the ethical standards and behavioral expectations for all employees, contractors, and representatives of YourExampleCompany. It reflects our commitment to integrity, professionalism, and mutual respect in all business activities.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to:
- All full-time and part-time employees
- Contract workers and temporary staff
- Interns and apprentices
- Board members and executives
- Consultants representing YourExampleCompany to clients

---

## 3. Core Values

### 3.1 Integrity
- MUST act honestly and transparently in all business dealings.
- MUST NOT misrepresent qualifications, capabilities, or deliverables to clients.
- MUST report any conflicts of interest to the immediate supervisor within 5 business days of discovery.

### 3.2 Professionalism
- MUST maintain a professional demeanor in all client interactions, whether in-person, virtual, or written.
- MUST dress appropriately for client engagements according to the client's workplace standards.
- SHOULD respond to internal communications within 1 business day.
- MUST respond to client communications within 4 business hours during working days.

### 3.3 Respect and Inclusion
- MUST treat all colleagues, clients, and partners with dignity and respect regardless of background.
- MUST NOT engage in discrimination, harassment, or bullying of any kind.
- SHOULD actively contribute to an inclusive workplace environment.
- MUST report witnessed harassment or discrimination to HR or use the anonymous reporting channel.

### 3.4 Confidentiality
- MUST NOT disclose client confidential information to unauthorized parties.
- MUST NOT discuss client projects in public spaces, social media, or with other clients.
- MUST follow the Information Security Policy (POL-005) for handling sensitive data.
- MUST return all client materials upon project completion or contract termination.

---

## 4. Workplace Behavior

### 4.1 Attendance and Punctuality
- MUST adhere to agreed-upon working hours and project schedules.
- MUST notify the team lead at least 2 hours before the start of the workday if unable to attend.
- SHOULD be available during core hours (10:00-16:00 local time) for team collaboration.

### 4.2 Communication
- MUST use company-approved communication channels for business discussions.
- MUST NOT use personal email accounts for client-related communications.
- SHOULD acknowledge receipt of important communications within 4 hours.

### 4.3 Substance Policy
- MUST NOT be under the influence of alcohol or illegal substances during working hours or client engagements.
- MUST NOT consume alcohol during business hours, including remote work.

---

## 5. Compliance and Reporting

### 5.1 Obligation to Report
- MUST report violations of this Code to the People & Culture Department or through the anonymous ethics hotline.
- MUST NOT retaliate against anyone who reports a violation in good faith.

### 5.2 Consequences
- Violations may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.
- Severity of consequences will be proportional to the nature and frequency of the violation.

---

## 6. Acknowledgment

All employees must acknowledge receipt and understanding of this Code of Conduct within 5 business days of their start date and annually thereafter.
