# Privacy Policy — Internal Guidelines

## Purpose

This policy establishes internal requirements for handling personal information in compliance with the Act on the Protection of Personal Information (APPI) and applicable international data protection laws.

## Collection

- Specify the purpose of use before collecting personal information
- Collect only the minimum information necessary for the specified purpose
- Obtain consent before collecting special care-required personal information (race, creed, medical history, criminal record, disability)
- Do not collect information by deception or improper means

## Use

- Use personal information only within the scope of the specified purpose
- Do not change the purpose of use beyond what is reasonably related to the original purpose without new consent
- Maintain accuracy of personal data and delete when no longer necessary

## Storage and Security

- Apply access controls proportionate to the sensitivity of the data
- Encrypt personal data at rest and in transit
- Restrict access to employees with a legitimate business need
- Log all access to personal data systems
- Conduct annual security assessments of personal data handling practices

## Third-Party Transfers

- Do not provide personal data to third parties without prior consent (unless legally required)
- Record all third-party transfers: date, recipient, items of data transferred
- For cross-border transfers: verify the recipient country's adequacy or obtain explicit consent
- Require contractual data protection commitments from third-party recipients

## Individual Rights

When individuals exercise their rights, respond within 2 weeks:
- **Disclosure**: Provide retained personal data in the requested format
- **Correction**: Investigate and correct inaccurate data
- **Deletion**: Delete data that was improperly collected or is no longer needed
- **Cessation**: Stop using data that was obtained improperly

## Breach Response

1. Contain the breach immediately
2. Assess the scope and potential impact
3. Notify the PPC and affected individuals without delay if the breach poses risk of harm
4. Document the incident, root cause, and corrective actions
5. Report to management within 24 hours

## Retention

- Retain personal data only as long as necessary for the specified purpose
- Follow the data retention schedule published by the Information Security team
- Securely destroy data that exceeds the retention period
