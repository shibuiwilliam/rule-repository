STATEMENT OF WORK #001

Pursuant to the Master Services Agreement dated March 1, 2025 between
Acme Solutions Inc. ("Provider") and GlobalTech Corp. ("Client").

Effective Date: April 1, 2025
Project Name: Customer Portal Modernization

1. SCOPE OF WORK
Provider shall design, develop, and deploy a modernized customer-facing web
portal comprising: (a) a responsive front-end application; (b) API integration
with Client's existing CRM and billing systems; (c) single-sign-on
authentication; and (d) an analytics dashboard for Client's operations team.

2. OUT OF SCOPE
The following are explicitly excluded: data migration from legacy systems,
mobile native applications, third-party license procurement, end-user training,
and ongoing production support beyond the warranty period.

3. DELIVERABLES AND MILESTONES

  Milestone 1 — Discovery & Design (Weeks 1–3)
    Deliverable: Requirements document and UI/UX wireframes
    Acceptance: Client review within 10 business days
    Payment: 20% of total fees ($40,000)

  Milestone 2 — Development Phase 1 (Weeks 4–8)
    Deliverable: Core portal with CRM integration
    Acceptance: Functional testing against acceptance criteria in Appendix A
    Payment: 30% of total fees ($60,000)

  Milestone 3 — Development Phase 2 (Weeks 9–12)
    Deliverable: SSO integration and analytics dashboard
    Acceptance: End-to-end testing per Appendix A
    Payment: 30% of total fees ($60,000)

  Milestone 4 — Deployment & Stabilization (Weeks 13–14)
    Deliverable: Production deployment and documentation
    Acceptance: 5 business days of production stability
    Payment: 20% of total fees ($40,000)

  Total Fees: $200,000 USD

5. CHANGE ORDERS
Any changes to scope, timeline, or fees require a written change order
signed by both Parties' project managers before work begins.

6. WARRANTY
Provider warrants deliverables against defects for forty-five (45) days
following final acceptance. Defects shall be corrected at no additional cost
within 5 business days of written notice.

7. GOVERNANCE
Weekly written status reports. Bi-weekly steering committee review at
milestones. Escalation: project manager level (5 business days), then
executive sponsor level (10 business days).
