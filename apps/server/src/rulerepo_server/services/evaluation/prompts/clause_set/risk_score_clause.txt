## Risk Factors to Evaluate
- Uncapped liability or indemnification
- Exclusive jurisdiction in counterparty's country
- IP assignment or broad license grants
- Unilateral termination rights favoring counterparty
- Automatic renewal with unfavorable terms
- Limitation of liability exclusions that leave gaps
- Non-compete or non-solicitation scope
- Data handling and cross-border transfer provisions
- Force majeure scope and notice requirements
- Warranty disclaimers that reduce counterparty obligations

## Instructions
1. Score the clause on each applicable risk factor (0.0 = no risk, 1.0 = maximum risk).
2. Calculate an overall risk score as the weighted average.
3. Flag any risk factors that exceed 0.7 as requiring legal review.
